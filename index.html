How It Works

Three steps from client call to signed document.

🎙️
Step 1

Record

Your client meeting is recorded and transcribed automatically via Read.ai. QuoteBot receives the transcript via webhook the moment the call ends.

🧠
Step 2

Extract

GPT-5.3 reads the transcript, extracts required services, matches them against your catalog, applies your rate tables, and generates pricing — complete with MSA discounts.

📄
Step 3

Generate

A fully branded SOW is generated as DOCX or PDF. Preview it in-browser, run through approval workflow, then email it to the client — all without leaving QuoteBot.

Everything You Need to Close Faster

From transcript to signed SOW — QuoteBot handles the heavy lifting so your team can focus on clients.

🤖

AI SOW Generation

Paste or auto-import a meeting transcript. GPT-5.3 extracts services, builds the project structure, and drafts a complete SOW in seconds.

💰

Smart Pricing Engine

Tiered rate tables, MSA discounts, and governance calculations applied automatically. AI pricing advice with market signals keeps you competitive.

📋

Service Catalog

Build and manage your full service catalog with categories. Sync directly from ScopeStack via OAuth2. Services are matched automatically during AI extraction.

🔌

ConnectWise Integration

Sync clients, push approved SOWs as opportunities, and track budget vs. actuals — all without leaving QuoteBot.

📊

Revenue Dashboard

KPI tracking, 6-month revenue pipeline, win/loss analytics, per-user activity reports, and full audit trail. Filter by any date range.

🔒

Enterprise Security

Microsoft Entra ID SSO, TOTP MFA, role-based access, NextAuth v5 sessions, and CrowdSec WAF protection. Built for production from day one.

📁

Document Workflow

DOCX and PDF generation with branded templates, in-browser preview, versioning, approval workflow, and direct email delivery.

⚡

Project Templates

Save winning project structures as templates. Copy and reuse them instantly. Combine multiple meeting transcripts into a single SOW for complex engagements.

🛡️

Multi-Model AI

Per-task model selection lets you dial in the right AI for each job — from fast synopsis generation to deep pricing analysis.
